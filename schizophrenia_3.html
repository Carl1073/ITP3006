Jealousy Delusion

A jealous delusion is a psychiatric disorder whereby a person believes, falsely, that their partner (or sexual partner) is being disloyal.

Cause of Jealousy Delusion

  • Mental Health Disorders:
    Anxiety Disorders cause people to fill insecurity and low self-esteem. This may contribute jealousy disorders by easily misinterpreting their partner's actions.
  • Past Experiences:
    Previous experiences of partner betrayals or infidelities can lead to over sensitive to potential threats.
  • Environment Influences:
    The person might be more prone to develop jealousy delusion if they live in surroundings where infidelity or exclusivity is common. likely to develop jealousy delusion.
  • Brain Abnormalities:
    Structural and functional changes in sectors of the brain including the insula and medial frontal/anterior cingulate cortex constitute brain abnormalities.
