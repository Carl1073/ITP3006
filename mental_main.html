Common Mental Disorders

Though each person's experience is different, millions of people worldwide suffer from mental health illnesses. Understanding such illnesses is the first step towards increasing awareness, empathy, and support for people affected. Here, we'll cover common mental health illnesses, their symptoms, and how to get assistance. For further information, please visit the link below.

  1. Schizophrenia and Psychotic Disorders

    Schizophrenia involves distorted thinking, perceptions, and emotions. It often includes psychosis, such as hallucinations or delusions. Below are the some of the types of Schizophrenia:
    • Persecutory
    • Grandiose
    • Jealousy
    • Erotomania

  2. Bipolar disorder

    Bipolar Disorder is characterized by extreme mood swings, ranging from manic (high energy, impulsivity) to depressive (low energy, sadness) episodes.

Seeking Help

If you or someone you know is suffering from any of these problems, it is critical to seek assistance. Mental health specialists including therapists, counselors, and psychiatrists can give necessary assistance and therapy. Remember that asking help displays strength, not weakness.
